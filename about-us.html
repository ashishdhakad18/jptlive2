Home About Us

Precision Machine Tools Built on Trust Since 1995

JPT Tools is a trusted name in machine tools and equipment, delivering reliable hand tools, power tools, and cordless solutions to professionals across India. With decades of experience, we understand the demands of modern workshops and industrial environments.

Our products are built to meet international standards of quality, safety, and performance. Every tool is designed with precision engineering and tested for durability, ensuring consistent results across every project.

Our Promise

To empower professionals with reliable, high-performance tools that simplify complex tasks and deliver consistent results. We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers through quality products, dependable service, and continuous innovation.

Our Story

JPT Tools began its journey in 1995 as a small machine tools shop with a clear vision—to provide reliable tools that professionals could depend on. Through consistent quality and dedication, we earned the trust of customers across industries.

As demand grew, so did our range. We expanded from traditional hand tools to advanced power and cordless tools, aligning with global manufacturing standards and modern engineering practices.

Today, JPT Tools stands as one of India’s leading suppliers, supported by a strong online platform that brings our complete catalog closer to customers. Our journey continues with the same passion—empowering every project with precision, pride, and performance.
