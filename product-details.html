FAQs

What's in the Box?
The package includes the pressure washer unit, a 21V battery, a charger, a 5m hose, a multi-spray nozzle, a foam pot, and a user manual.
How does it work?
Simply connect the hose to a water source (like a bucket or tap), attach the nozzle, and pull the trigger. The motor pumps water at high pressure for effective cleaning.
What is the warranty?
This product comes with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
How to use?
Assemble according to the manual, ensure the battery is charged, and you're ready to clean!
What is the maximum pressure (PSI)?
Our cordless pressure washer delivers a peak pressure of up to 450 PSI, perfect for cars, decks, and outdoor furniture.
Battery capacity and runtime?
It features a 21V 4.0Ah Lithium-ion battery that provides approximately 30-40 minutes of continuous runtime on a full charge.
Is the motor brushless?
Yes, it uses an advanced high-efficiency brushless motor that offers longer life and 50% more runtime compared to brushed motors.
What is the flow rate?
The maximum flow rate is 4.0 Liters per minute (LPM), ensuring efficient water usage while maintaining cleaning power.
Low water pressure?
Check if the hose is kinked or if the filter in the intake is clogged. Ensure the water source is sufficient and the battery is fully charged.
Machine won't start?
Ensure the battery is firmly inserted and charged. Check the trigger lock and make sure the safety switch is engaged.
Battery won't charge?
Verify that the charger is connected to a working power outlet and that the charging pins are clean and free from debris.
Water leaking from the hose?
Check the O-rings and quick-connect fittings. Ensure they are pushed in securely and not damaged. Tighten all connections.
