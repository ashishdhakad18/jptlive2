Warranty, Shipping, Returns & Refund Policy

At JPT Tools, we are committed to providing high-quality products and a transparent, reliable customer experience.

This document outlines our policies on warranty, returns, refunds, cancellations, and shipping to ensure fairness and clarity for every transaction.

Coverage

JPT Tools provides a warranty against manufacturing defects only, effective from the date of invoice.

1. The warranty covers motor-related defects or faults due to manufacturing or material issues.

2. Different models may carry different warranty durations as mentioned on their product pages.

3. Warranty services are provided directly by JPT Tools (for JPT-branded products only).

Exclusions

The warranty does not cover:

1. Accessories, attachments, or consumable parts (such as brushes, bits, batteries, or charger).

2. Damage caused by misuse, mishandling, transportation, accidents, or negligence.

3. Normal wear and tear, rusting, or physical damage.

4. Electrical surges, improper voltage, or incompatible power use.

5. Water, moisture, or heat damage.

Repair & Service Conditions

JPT provides only carry-in warranty. Customer need to present their product to the JPT authorized service center.

Pick-up drop service is available on the serviceable pincodes accross the India (chargeable basis under warranty or out of warranty)

All repairs are subject to inspection and approval by the JPT Tools service team.

Claim Process

If you face an issue covered under warranty:

Contact Customer Care via WhatsApp (+91 907-450-8686) or email (support@jpttools.com).

Provide your purchase invoice and a clear video or photo of the issue.

Our team will verify your claim and guide you through the next steps.

Warranty repair or replacement typically takes 10–14 business days, depending on part availability.

Warranty for Bosch Products

As an authorized Bosch dealer, JPT Tools liability is limited to selling genuine Bosch products.

All warranty-related services, claims, or repairs for Bosch products are handled exclusively by Bosch under their official warranty policy.

All warranty-related services, claims, or repairs for Bosch products are handled exclusively by Bosch. Customer need to contact the Bosch support team for further details.

Shipping Policy

Our shipping process is designed to deliver your products safely, quickly, and efficiently, with complete visibility and accountability

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation (for pre-paid orders). The delivery time depends on your location and courier availability.

Delivery Time

1. Standard delivery: 4–7 business days within India.

2. Rural or remote areas may take longer.

3. Tracking details will be shared via email or WhatsApp once shipped.

Damaged Packages

If your parcel arrives damaged:

Record an unboxing video and contact Customer Care immediately. Claims without proper proof (video/photos) may not be accepted.

Important Note:- Start recording an unboxing video before opening the parcel, showing all six sides of the package. After opening, display all items, accessories, and any visible defects clearly in the video.

If the video does not follow these steps, the claim will not be accepted.

Order Cancellation Policy

We offer flexible and transparent cancellation options, allowing changes before dispatch and fair refunds for cancelled orders.

Pre-shipment

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch at no charge. Contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp with your order details.

Post-shipment

Once shipped, cancellation is not possible. You may refuse delivery or initiate a return after delivery.

If the order is a commercial one, the shipping charges for both sending and receiving will be deducted. All the details will be shared by our team through WhatsApp.

Company-initiated

If JPT Tools cancels an order (due to stock issues or other reasons), you’ll be notified and refunded (If pre-paid order) in full within 5–7 business days from where the transaction initiate.

Return Policy

Our return and exchange policy ensures peace of mind for customers who receive damaged, incorrect, or defective items.

Return Eligibility

Orders can be returned before dispatch at no charge. Contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp with your order details.

Return Process

Once shipped, cancellation is not possible. You may refuse delivery or initiate a return after delivery.

If the order is a commercial one, the shipping charges for both sending and receiving will be deducted. All the details will be shared by our team through WhatsApp.

Return Time

If JPT Tools cancels an order (due to stock issues or other reasons), you’ll be notified and refunded (If pre-paid order) in full within 5–7 business days from where the transaction initiate.
